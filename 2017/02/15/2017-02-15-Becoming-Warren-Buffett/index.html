Becoming Warren Buffett

2017-02-15 Books

HBO出品 Becoming Warren Buffett -《成为沃伦巴菲特》
Warren认识Bill Gate没多久，Bill Gate的父亲要Bill和Warren在一张纸上写一个词来描述对他们帮助最大的是什么。他们事先没有沟通过，但他们同时都写下了一个词：Focus。

** Focus is always been a strong part of my personality, by getting interested in something, I get really interested. **

最大的感触就是老头都要87了，还是这么神采奕奕，思维敏捷，口齿伶俐，精力旺盛！

另外，真的是越有钱的人生活越节俭。一栋老宅一住就是50多年，清晨路过麦当劳每天早晨的早餐多少钱就记得清清楚楚。

还有这老头这么爱喝可口可乐呀，到哪都是一杯。

47:02
“I like to sit and think, and I spend a lot of time doing that. Sometimes it’s just pretty unproductive, but I find it enjoyable to think about business and investment problems. They are easy, it’s the human problems that are the tough ones. Sometimes there aren’t any good answers with human problems, there is always good answer with money. “

53:31
“The biggest thing in making money is time. You don’t have to be particularly smart. You just have to be patient”

59:04
“It takes 20 years to build the reputation, and it takes 5 minutes to lose it.”
